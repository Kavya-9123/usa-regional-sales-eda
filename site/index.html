Key Insights

🏆

Product concentration

The top ~10% of products drive over 60% of revenue and 55% of profit margin. Product 26, 25 and 13 lead.

📍

Regional performance

California leads with ~$230M revenue and 7,500+ orders; Illinois, Florida and Texas follow at ~$85M–$110M.

🔀

Channel mix

Wholesale ~54%, Distributors ~31%, Exports ~15% — a clear opportunity to scale international presence.

Risk flags
