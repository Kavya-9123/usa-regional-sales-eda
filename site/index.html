Key Insights

Product concentration: The top ~10% of products drive over 60% of total revenue and 55% of profit margin. Product 26, 25 and 13 lead the portfolio.
Regional performance: California leads with ~$230M revenue and 7,500+ orders; Illinois, Florida and Texas follow at ~$85M–$110M.
Channel mix: Wholesale ~54%, Distributors ~31%, Exports ~15% — a clear opportunity to scale international presence.
Margins: Profit margins range broadly from ~18% to ~60% with no strong correlation to unit price, suggesting standardized pricing tiers.
Risk flags: Over 200 high-risk outlier transactions were flagged for pricing anomalies or margins below 5%.
