## 📌 Project Overview

This project explores a year of U.S. regional sales data (**2025**) across multiple product lines, sales channels, and regions. The goal is to identify revenue drivers, surface regional and channel trends, and detect anomalies through **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**, an **interactive Power BI report**, and a **live web dashboard**.

---

## 🌐 Live Dashboard

The interactive web dashboard (HTML + Chart.js) is deployed on Vercel:

**➡️ https://usa-regional-sales-eda.vercel.app**

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales data across 6 product lines, 4 sales channels, and 8 U.S. regions.
- Identify **top-performing products** contributing the majority of revenue and profit.
- Break down **regional and channel performance**.
- Detect **high-risk transactions** and pricing anomalies.
- Present insights through both static analysis and an interactive dashboard.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- **Language:** Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn, Chart.js
- **Dashboarding:** Power BI, HTML/CSS
- **Deployment:** Vercel
- **IDE:** Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

- The **top 10% of products** contribute over **60% of total revenue** and **55% of profit margin**.
- **California** leads regional performance (~$230M revenue, 7,500+ orders).
- **Channel mix:** Wholesale ~54%, Distributors ~31%, Exports ~15%.
- **200+ high-risk outlier transactions** flagged for pricing anomalies or margins below 5%.
